\section{Measure Uncertainties}
Experimental variance (unbiased):
\begin{equation*}
  s^{2}(x) = \dfrac{1}{N-1} \sum_{k=1}^{N}{(x_{k} - \bar{x})}^{2}
\end{equation*}

Standard uncertainty of the mean of all $N$ samples:
\begin{equation*}
  u(\bar{x}) = \dfrac{\sqrt{s^{2}(x)}}{\sqrt{N}}
\end{equation*}
